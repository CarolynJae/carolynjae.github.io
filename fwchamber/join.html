Non-Profit-$100

As a Chamber, we understand the importance of supporting our non-profit community partners. If you are a 501(c)(3), this package offers your organization a Bronze-level membership at a reduced rate.

Bronze-$200

This essential membership for home-based businesses and other “solo-preneurs” includes the following benefits:
• 12-month Chamber membership
• Membership plaque and decal
• Basic listing in the online Business Directory
• Ribbon cutting/Grand Opening event
• Networking opportunities at all Chamber events
• Opportunity to serve on committees
• Business Cards and brochures at Chamber office lobby
• Receive Weekly e-Newsletter
• Free notary public

Silver-$400

This community supporter membership includes the following benefits:
• All benefits of a Bronze-level member, PLUS:
• Enhanced listing in the online Business Directory
• 2 additional categories in the Business Directory
• 6 e-Blasts to all Chamber members
• 12 months of category online advertising on the Chamber website
• Plaque recognizing membership level

Gold-$1000

This legacy membership includes the following benefits:
• All benefits of a Bronze-level Member, PLUS:
• Enhanced listing in the online Business Directory
• Up to 5 categories in the Business Directory
• 12 e-Blasts to all Chamber members
• 12 months of category online advertising on the Chamber website
• Opportunity to post coupons or discounts on Chamber website for free
• Your business logo and link included at bottom of every weekly e-Newsletter
• Signage in the Chamber office promoting Executive Members
• Business logo and link displayed on home page of Chamber website
• Inclusion in event programs as a Gold-level Member
• Early bird opportunity for exclusive sponsorships at our signature events
• Plaque recognizing membership level
