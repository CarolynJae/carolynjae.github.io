Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Doen
doen.com

The Doen website does a fabulous job with viusal hierarchy. The main picture is large and at the top. The next thing you notice is the form to sign up for their mailing list. All other information on the page is smaller, but still easily accessible.

Alignment

Udemy
udemy.com

Udemy has many online classes available and they have some of the most popular ones listed right on their homepage. The grid-system with a strong vertical and horizontal alignment maes everything clean and organized so that it is easy to find whatever you are looking for.

White Space and Clean Design

Farmhouse on Boone
farmhouseonboone.com

This is one of my favorite blogs to follow. Although there is a lot of information on the home page, each picture is given space to breathe which give it a clean and simple feel - much like the blog itself.
