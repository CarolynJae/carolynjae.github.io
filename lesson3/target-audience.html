Target Audience Residents in the area concerned about the weather Homeschool parents with kids ages 6-12 who want to do a weather unit for science class High School students looking into meteorology as a possible career Personas: Melanie Mills (34) – homeschool mom of 4 kids Molly (6), Marky (4), Mimi (3), and Mo (1) – uses website as a resource for science lessons– Molly loves playing her games after they finish their lessons for the day. Jan Storm – 17-year-old High School student at local high school – fascinated with weather and wants to be a meteorologist when she grows up. Sometimes she likes to go out in bad weather to practice live weather updates. Scenarios: Is it a good day to play at the park? When will the rain stop so I can go for a run? Why is it snowing in May? How do clouds form? How do you know where to point when using a green screen to do the weather?
